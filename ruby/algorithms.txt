Teaching your robot to use an elevator.

1. verify what room you want to go to.
2. verify what floor the room is on.
3. approach the elevator
4. check what floor you are currently on
5. press the up button if you need to go up, down button if you need to go down
6. go to room if you are on the correct floor
7. enter the elevator
8. select the correct floor for the room
9. wait for elevator to take you to the selected floor
10. exit the elevator -> #6.



Getting a drink of water.

1. approach the kitchen
2. open up the drinking glass cupboard
3. select a drinking glass
4. verify it is clean
5. go to kitchen sink with glass
6. fill glass to desired amount of water
7. drink water
8. leave glass in sink
